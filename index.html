Stéphanie Challita

"Everything you can imagine is real." -- Pablo Picasso
I am an Associate Professor at University of Rennes.
I am part of DiverSE research team (joint to Irisa and Inria Rennes - Bretagne Atlantique), and I teach at ESIR.

My research interests include:
  • Model-Driven Engineering,
  • Software Language Engineering,
  • Reverse-Engineering,
  • Model Verification & Validation,
  • Cloud Computing,
  • and Internet of Things.

  • 2020-Present:    Associate Professor
                           ESIR, Irisa laboratory & Inria Rennes - Bretagne Atlantique, DiverSE project-team – Rennes, France
                                  
  • 2019-2020:    Postdoctoral Researcher
                           I3S laboratory & Inria Sophia Antipolis – Méditerranée, Kairos project-team – Valbonne, France
                                  
  • 2015-2018:    PhD in Computer Science

  • 2014-2015:    Research Master’s degree in Computer Science
